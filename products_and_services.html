Synergy's products & services:

Quality Appraisals, Review & More

Synergy Appraisal Services offers an array of residential valuations services for asset management, forensic review, litigation, loan QC, marketing, mortgage lending, repurchase negotiation and tax appeal purposes. We specialize in providing high-quality appraisals and reviews, prompt service and competitive pricing.

Review Expertise
For customers performing loan QC or negotiating repurchase demands, we have a specialized panel of reviewers with expertise in forensic valuation services. We offer retrospective field reviews to determine the reliability of an origination appraisal report (OAR), as well as to evaluate the reasonableness of repurchase demands. In addition, our comprehensive desk reconciliation review and field reconciliation review products can critique multiple valuation services, reconcile them to a well-supported value opinion, and be used effectively during negotiations to reduce losses from repurchase demands.

Synergy Appraisal Services employs a quality control (QC) process to ensure that our customers receive well-supported valuation services which are properly reported. Every report we produce undergoes our QC Completeness Check to ensure the client’s quality standards have been met before the report is delivered.
