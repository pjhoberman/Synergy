A History of Synergy:

Meet Our Professionals

Founder & CEO David Dill david@synergyappraisal.com

David Dill was born and raised in Michigan. He attended Oakland University in Rochester, MI, where he majored in Marketing and Finance. David loves his home state and still spends much of his time there, although he travels frequently to visit Customers in their locations. Centering his actions around Customers has been a lifelong focus for David. After college, David worked as a Private Wealth Manager for Huntington National Bank. It was his passion for delivering excellent Customer Service that powered David's success. His banking clients, and family's encouragement, inspired him to become a young entreprenuer. Read More

David incorporated Synergy Appraisal Services sixteen years ago with one simple premise: To provide exceptional Customer Service that is uniquely designed to meet the specific business needs of each Client. David's philosophy is to develop Win-Win-Win solutions, which are sustainable, to deliver exceptional results for Customers.

Understanding that service is only part of a winning formula for his Customers, David also became an early advocate of implementing a quality control function to mitigate risk and strengthen credit quality. It is no mystery why David's passion for excellent service and consistent quality has made him very successful and endeared him to Customers.

PresidentJames Baumberger jim@synergyappraisal.com

Born in Ohio, Jim is a second generation appraiser with decades of successful management experience. His 21 years of professional experience includes valuation services for asset management, eminent domain, lending, litigation, loan quality control, marketing, and relocation purposes. He regularly consults with both staff and fee appraisers regarding complex property valuations.Read More

Jim has an outstanding record of providing senior leadership for appraisal organizations. Some of his past positions include Collateral Services Manager for Washington Mutual, Regional Chief Appraiser for Bank of America, Division Appraisal Manager for World Savings, and Mortgage Group Leader for Wachovia. In addition, Jim has taught appraisal licensing and continuing education courses for many years. He also develops and presents training seminars on subject matter including appraisal, compliance, market conditions, review practice, and secondary market guidelines. Jim particularly enjoys talking with other professionals about changes affecting real estate markets, as well as changes affecting professional appraisal practice.

Jim understands that the only constant in business is change and this is especially true in the real estate industry today. He makes it a point to keep Synergy's Clients informed of the latest developments impacting our industry. No matter the regulatory changes to come, or the compliance efforts required, Jim is well prepared to help our valued Clients better understand how to effectively mitigate financial and reputational risks by implementing compliant processes.

JAMES MORRIS National Accounts Manager jmorris@synergyappraisal.com

Read More about James

Joined the Synergy Family in July of 2011 after having spent almost six years managing appraisals for a huge AMC. My educational background is in neuroscience and philosophy, especially applied ethics. From that perspective, I have always found the appraisal business fascinating and always taken pride in working with industry professionals. I believe we work in an economic sector that directly impacts the life of everyone in some way and, therefore, it is imperative I do the best I can to facilitate the highest quality reporting and service possible for our clients. And I believe this can be accomplished by being respectful and professional to all parties involved – whether they are a homeowner, loan officer, processor, or appraiser. My main role with the Synergy Family is to lead our team of Account Managers. I help the Account Managers develop solutions when complex issues arise in our workflow. If you have a yurt on 50 acres in Bozeman, MT, I will make calls to help your Account Manager find the most qualified local appraiser. Does your underwriter need a comp within 90 days and the appraiser says there isn't one available in the last six months? I will work with the appraiser and our QC team to arrive at the best possible outcome for our Client. I believe that working as a team – both internally here at Synergy and with our Clients – we can achieve much better outcomes than by working alone. When I am not working, I do percussion and backup vocals in an indie rock band in Southeast Michigan, where I call Ann Arbor home. I love the city of Detroit, despite its recent challenges, and admire the wonderful people working here to rebuild this beautiful, vibrant city. I'm a Michigan transplant, who has grown deep roots in the local community. Although I was born in Florida, raised in West Virginia and attended college in Ohio, Michigan has been a wonderful home these last seven years. Aside from banging drums and singing and visiting the city, I enjoy video games, reading about current events and politics (don't get me started), and spending time with family and friends. I'm proud to work with David and Jim and the rest of our Synergy team striving to make our industry better for all involved.
