A History of Synergy:

Meet Our Professionals

Founder & CEO David Dill david@synergyappraisal.com

David was born and raised in Michigan and attended Oakland University in Rochester, where he majored in Marketing and Finance. David loves his home state and still spends much of his time there although he travels frequently to visit clients. Centering his actions around customers has been a lifelong focus for David. After college, he worked as a Private Wealth Manager for Huntington National Bank. It was his passion for delivering excellent customer service that powered David's success. His banking clients and family's encouragement inspired him to become a young entrepreneur. Read More

David incorporated Synergy Appraisal Services sixteen years ago with the simple premise of providing exceptional Customer Service that is uniquely designed to meet the specific business needs of each client. His philosophy is to develop sustainable Win-Win-Win solutions that deliver exceptional results for customers.

Understanding that service is only part of a winning formula for his customers, David also became an early advocate of implementing a quality control function to mitigate risk and strengthen credit quality. It's no mystery why David's passion for excellent service and consistent quality has made him successful and endeared him to customers.

PresidentJames Baumberger jim@synergyappraisal.com

Born in Ohio, Jim is a second generation appraiser with decades of successful management experience. His 21 years of professional experience includes valuation services for asset management, eminent domain, lending, litigation, loan quality control, marketing, and relocation purposes. He regularly consults with both staff and fee appraisers regarding complex property valuations.Read More

Jim has an outstanding record of providing senior leadership for appraisal organizations with past positions Some of his past positions at Washington Mutual, Bank of America, World Savings and Wachovia. In addition, Jim has taught appraisal licensing and continuing education courses for many years, and he also develops and presents training seminars. Jim particularly enjoys talking with other professionals about changes affecting real estate markets, as well as changes affecting professional appraisal practice.

Jim understands that the only constant in business is change and this is especially true in the real estate industry today. He makes it a point to keep Synergy's clients informed of the latest developments impacting our industry. No matter the regulatory changes to come, or the compliance efforts required, Jim is well prepared to help our valued clients better understand how to effectively mitigate financial and reputational risks by implementing compliant processes.
