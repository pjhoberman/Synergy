Working with Synergy:

The Intellipanel System

The most important aspect of effective panel management is hiring intelligently. We excel at finding local appraisers with geographic competency, property knowledge and technical proficiency. Employing expertise up-front to engage the right appraiser for our client’s specific property mitigates many of the subsequent challenges—like service delays and revision requests—that plague poorly placed orders.

Synergy's recruiters are proven experts at finding the right appraisers for complex properties and difficult markets. Our staff thrives on solving challenges and providing sound advice so clients can make better business decisions.

Our intelligent panel management is designed to deliver valuation services to our customers in a timely manner at competitive prices. Our appraisers perform exceptionally well for our customers because we also add value to their business. Appraisers are paid promptly twice each month and we also handle marketing, relieving them of many auxillary business responsibilities. Our QC Managers are eager to consult with appraisers to help them solve challenging valuation problems. In addition, our expert account managers treat appraisers with the professional courtesy and respect they deserve.

Just as Synergy Appraisal Services values long-term, mutually rewarding business relationships with our clients, we also value long-term working relationships with our appraisers. Synergy is an active service partner. We help appraisers resolve challenges, improve quality, increase production and speed-up service for our customers.
