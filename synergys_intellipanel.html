Working with Synergy:

The Intellipanel System

The most important aspect of effective Panel Management we perform for our Customers is hiring intelligently: Synergy Appraisal Services excels at hiring local appraisers with the geographic competency, property knowledge, and technical proficiency to perform high-quality valuation services. This reflects the belief that employing our expertise up-front to engage the right appraiser for our Client’s specific property mitigates many of the subsequent challenges, service delays, and revision requests that plague poorly placed orders.

Synergy distinguishes itself from the competition by effectively leveraging our panel of expert appraisers to tackle the most difficult valuation assignments. Synergy has best-in-class recruiters with proven experience at finding the right appraisers for the most complex properties in the most difficult markets. Our staff thrives on solving challenges and providing sound advice, so Clients can make better informed business decisions. Our QC Managers are integral to the recruiting process, helping to ensure we engage only the most competent appraisers.

Our intelligent Panel Management is designed to deliver high-quality, compliant valuation services to our Customers, in a timely manner at competitive prices, to mitigate risk and ensure better business decisions. Our appraisers perform exceptionally well for our Customers, because we also add value to their business.

We pay our appraisers compliant fees, promptly twice each month, so they do not have to collect receivables. We perform the marketing function for our appraisers, relieving them of sales and business development responsibilities. Our QC Managers are eager to consult with appraisers, to help them solve challenging valuation problems. In addition, our expert Account Managers treat appraisers with the professional courtesy and respect they deserve.

Just as Synergy Appraisal Services values long-term, mutually-rewarding business relationships with our Clients, we also value long-term, cordial working relationships with our appraisers. We want to be the best in the business, so we strive to be helpful. Synergy Appraisal Services is an active service partner for our appraisers. We help them resolve challenges, improve quality, increase production, and speed-up service for our Customers.
