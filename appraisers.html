Attitude: Our attitude is simple. We want to be helpful; we want to be the best in the business! Synergy Appraisal Services exists to serve our Clients and support our Appraisers. Delivering good service to Clients is something you strive for every day in your business. Synergy Appraisal Services is dedicated to helping you succeed. Compensation: You, as the Appraiser, are the key to our mutual success. We do this by operating efficiently to minimize our administrative, management, and technology costs. This enables us to pay you competitive fees. We are not merely seeking an appraisal from you; we are seeking a long-term, mutually profitable business relationship. You should be rewarded with reasonable fees and repeat business for the good work you contribute!

Ethics: Synergy Appraisal Services believes in the fundamental importance of an ethical, independent real estate valuation services profession. The credit, economic, and housing crises that have adversely impacted our nation over the last several years reinforce the importance of having ethical Appraisers in the real estate and lending industries. We comply fully with Dodd-Frank's Section 1472, "Appraiser Independence" provisions. Our compliant processes ensure you are not coerced or unduly influenced by anyone during the appraisal process.
